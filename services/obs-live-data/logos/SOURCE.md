# Team logos

These team logo PNGs are bundled from the **ssl-status-board** project:
https://github.com/RoboCup-SSL/ssl-status-board (`frontend/src/assets/logos/`).

Filenames are the team name lowercased with spaces replaced by hyphens, matching the
ssl-game-controller team name (e.g. `tigers-mannheim.png`, `er-force.png`). `no-logo.png`
is the fallback used when a team has no matching logo.
